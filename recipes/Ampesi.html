Ampesi and Kontomire

DESCRIPTION

A local Ghanaian dish which made from boiled palantain andd yam

Ampesi

INGREDIENTS

  • Yams,plantains
  • Water
  • Salt

STEPS FOR PREPARING AMPESI

  1. Slice the yam and plantaians
  2. Boil the sliced yam and plantains for 15 minutes
  3. Serve in plate

Kontomire steaw

INGREDIENTS

  • Cocoyam leaves
  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • Palm oil
  • Proteins
  • Local proteins(kobi and Moni)

STEPS FOR THE PREPARATION OF KONTOMIRE STEW

  1. Heat the palm oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the momoni and half of the sliced onions.
  3. Fry until aromatic and translucent.
  4. Stir in the blended pepper, ginger, garlic, turkey berries, and remaining onions.
  5. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the tomatoes and cook down until the oil begins to separate from the sauce.
  7. Gently pour in the egusi paste without stirring immediately.
  8. Cover the pot and let it simmer for 3 to 5 minutes so the egusi curdles into small chunks resembling scrambled eggs.
  9. Stir gently.
  10. Add your proteins: Stir in the flaked smoked salmon, canned mackerel, and pre-boiled koobi.
  11. Let it simmer for 5 minutes so the flavors blend.
  12. Incorporate the greens: Add the steamed kontomire leaves.
  13. Mix well, lower the heat, and allow it to simmer for another 5 minutes.
  14. Taste and adjust for salt.
