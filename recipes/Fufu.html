Fufu

DESCRIPTION

A local ghanaian dish made from cassava and plantain

INGREDIENTS

  • Cassava tubers
  • Unriped Plantain
  • Water

STEPS

  1. Peel and boil the cassava and plantains until soft
  2. Drain the water and let it cool down slightly
  3. Pound them together in a mortar with a pistle, adding drops of water
  4. Shape it into rounds once its stretchy
